Spaghetti Carbonara Recipe

Description:

Spaghetti Carbonara is a classic Italian pasta dish made with eggs, cheese, pancetta, and black pepper. It's a simple yet incredibly delicious recipe that's perfect for a quick and satisfying meal.

Ingredients:

  • 8 oz (225g) spaghetti
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup (100g) Pecorino Romano cheese, grated
  • 4 oz (110g) pancetta or guanciale, diced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • Freshly ground black pepper

Steps:

  1. Cook the spaghetti in a large pot of salted boiling water until al dente.
  2. While the pasta is cooking, saut√© the pancetta or guanciale in a pan until crispy. Add the minced garlic and cook for an additional minute.
  3. In a bowl, whisk together the eggs, grated Pecorino Romano cheese, and a generous amount of freshly ground black pepper.
  4. When the pasta is done, drain it, reserving a cup of pasta cooking water.
  5. Combine the hot drained pasta with the pancetta and garlic mixture, tossing to coat the pasta in the rendered fat.
  6. Remove the pan from heat and quickly pour the egg and cheese mixture over the pasta. Toss vigorously to create a creamy sauce, using reserved pasta cooking water if needed to achieve the desired consistency.
  7. Serve immediately, garnished with extra Pecorino Romano cheese and black pepper.
