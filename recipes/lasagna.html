Lasagna Recipe

Description:

This delicious lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel sauce, and plenty of cheese. It's a perfect comfort food for any occasion.

Ingredients:

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef or sausage
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Crushed tomatoes
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Eggs
  • Italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper

Steps:

  1. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  2. In a pan, brown the ground beef or sausage with chopped onion and garlic.
  3. Add crushed tomatoes, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper to the meat sauce and simmer.
  4. In a bowl, mix together ricotta cheese, eggs, and Parmesan cheese.
  5. Layer lasagna noodles, meat sauce, cheese mixture, and mozzarella cheese in a baking dish.
  6. Repeat the layers until the dish is full, finishing with a layer of mozzarella on top.
  7. Bake in the oven at 375°F (190°C) for about 30-35 minutes or until the cheese is bubbly and golden.
  8. Let it cool for a few minutes before serving.
